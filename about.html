About

Hi, My name is Gabriel Awosusi (A.K.A., Greatonic). I am a Nigerian. I have been into Arts, mostly in the aspect of sketching and drawing since I was 7 years old. During December, 2017, I came across a digital painting program (PaintTool SAI) and this brought out my thirst in computer graphics. Since then, I have taken time to practice not only on my digital art skill but also learned a few other skills as well (see more in Services...) after a lot of dedication, practice, hardwork, and prayer. I believe learning never ends and so I still continue to learn everyday because "Knowledge Is Power" and not the other way around. Peace!.
